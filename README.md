# Automated-e-mail-web-app-using-Google-App-Script
This an online automated direct mail where yo can personalize the e-mail you will send and to who it will be sent. The e-mail adresses must have commas between them and you can edit the color, the size of the words inside the e-mail, different types of greetings and the alignment of the text (justify, center, left and right). Please, enjoy it!
